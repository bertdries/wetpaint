Description:
'WET PAINT' is a bold, multi-layered silkscreen artwork by Musketon, combining technical precision with sculptural detail and political satire. Printed in 10 distinct colors, this piece features a striking hair treatment using specialty gold flake ink—a shimmering, reflective finish that catches the light and reinforces the absurd theatricality of the subject: Donald J. Trump.

A unique custom-made 3D printed painter is fixed inside the frame, positioned mid-action as he paints Trump entirely in orange. This miniature sculptural detail bridges the gap between two-dimensional print and physical object, creating a hybrid work that challenges the boundaries of traditional printmaking.

The frame is custom built in Belgium using high-grade pine wood and fitted with museum-grade Artglass™, ensuring both elegance and archival quality. Each piece is ready to hang, complete with integrated mounting hardware for effortless installation.

The work is hand-signed by Musketon on the front, and includes a holographic, signed and numbered certificate on the back of the piece. A second certificate of authenticity with full artwork details is included in a sealed envelope.

To emphasize exclusivity and personal connection, each piece is personally hand-delivered to your door within Belgium by Musketon himself.

This artwork is a fusion of satire, craftsmanship, and collectibility—designed to provoke, amuse, and endure.
